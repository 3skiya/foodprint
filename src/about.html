About

The FoodPrint Journey

FoodPrint is a food & agriculture startup based out of Cape Town, South Africa. It is building traceability platform for producers and customers focused on sustainbly grown and organic produce.

Spurred on by the the need to know more about the source of our foods, the team makes use of the latest technology to capture the journey of produce from the fields to the markets

The initial pilot phase is taking place at OZCF Farm and Market. The market's patrons are able to access information regarding selected produce from their phones.

As the platform grows, more producers will be added to the cohort and consumers will have more information to base their consumption choices on.
