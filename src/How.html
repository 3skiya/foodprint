How it works?

The FoodPrint Produce Journey.

Farm

The local market farmers register with FoodPrint and grow their tasty fresh produce. The range of produce includes varies from Cara Cara oranges to leafy greens.

Harvest

Farmers log into their profile and log their latest harvest produce. The produce is stored in the blockchain to ensure transparency of its origin.

Transportation & Storage

The produce is carefully packed and transported to the Broker / Market. It is logged into the FoodPrint system and kept fresh in cold storage. No plastic packaging is used in this process, ensuring our environmental sustainability.

Market Day

On Market morning, you pick the produce you'd like. Simply scan the QR code next to your chosen fruit/veg and see where it's from, when it was picked and whether it's organic.
